# Privacy Policy for PatternPulse

**Last updated: January 2025**

## Overview

PatternPulse is a Chrome extension that helps you practice algorithmic pattern recognition on LeetCode. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

### What We Store

PatternPulse stores the following data **locally in your browser** using Chrome's storage API:

- Problems you've completed
- Patterns you've identified
- Number of hints used per problem
- Your streak count

### What We Don't Collect

- No personal information (name, email, etc.)
- No browsing history
- No analytics or tracking
- No data sent to external servers

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using `chrome.storage.local`. This data:

- Never leaves your browser
- Is not accessible to us or any third party
- Is deleted if you uninstall the extension
- Can be exported or reset from the extension popup

## Permissions

PatternPulse requires the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `activeTab` | To detect when you're on a LeetCode problem page |
| `storage` | To save your progress locally |
| `scripting` | To inject the quiz UI on LeetCode pages |
| `host_permissions: leetcode.com` | To run only on LeetCode |

## Third-Party Services

PatternPulse does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

## Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

- **Export**: Use the "Export Data" button in the extension popup
- **Delete**: Use the "Reset" button or uninstall the extension
- **View**: All data is visible in the extension popup

---

**TL;DR**: Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal information.
